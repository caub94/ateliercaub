The aim of the project was the renovation of an office building in the Hague. Due to a great demand for living space the decision to transform office space into residential space was obvious. To achieve a sustainable solution to a project that the building might soon be demolished reusable wooden CLT pots were inserted into the existing building structure - self servicing units that could be reused in later projects.

Location: City Centre | The Hague

Size: Building | 12.148 m2

Year: 1999 / 2019
